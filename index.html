Welcome to my website to learn more about my research and teaching activities!
Lynda Chehami is an Associate Professor at Université Polytechnique Hauts-de-France and INSA Hauts-de-France. She works on ultrasonic wave propagation, passive imaging, and wave correlation in complex and reverberant media, for the characterization of thin films, multilayer systems, and advanced materials. She is a member of CNU 63, Chair of the ULTRASOUNDS technical committee of the European Acoustics Association (EEA), and is responsible for the Physics-Chemistry curriculum for the licence degree.

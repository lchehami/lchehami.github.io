Welcome to my website

I am an Associate Professor HDR in Physical Acoustics at Université Polytechnique Hauts-de-France and INSA Hauts-de-France. My research focuses on ultrasonic wave propagation, passive imaging, and wave correlation in complex and reverberant media, with applications to the characterization of thin films, multilayer systems, and advanced materials.

Lynda Chehami, HDR – Associate Professor in Physical Acoustics

Université Polytechnique Hauts-de-France INSA Hauts-de-France IEMN – site de Valenciennes, CNRS UMR 8520 CNRS
Welcome to my website to learn more about my research and teaching activities!

I am Lynda Chehami, HDR Associate Professor in Physical Acoustics at Université Polytechnique Hauts-de-France (UPHF), INSA Hauts-de-France, and the IEMN CNRS UMR 8520 laboratory. My research focuses on ultrasonic guided waves, passive imaging, ambient-noise interferometry, wave correlation, coda-wave analysis, structural health monitoring, thin films, multilayer structures, and advanced materials characterization. I currently serve as Chair of the Ultrasounds Technical Committee of the European Acoustics Association (EAA).

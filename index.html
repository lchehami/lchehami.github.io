Welcome to my website to learn more about my research and teaching activities!

Lynda Chehami is an Associate Professor at Université Polytechnique Hauts-de-France and INSA Hauts-de-France. She works on ultrasonic wave propagation, passive imaging, and wave correlation in complex and reverberant media, for the characterization of thin films, multilayer systems, and advanced materials.

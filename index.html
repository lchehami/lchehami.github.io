Lynda Chehami – Associate Professor HDR in Physical Acoustics

Université Polytechnique Hauts-de-France INSA Hauts-de-France IEMN – site de Valenciennes, CNRS UMR 8520 CNRS
Welcome to my website to learn more about my research and teaching activities!

I am an Associate Professor (HDR) in Physical Acoustics at Université Polytechnique Hauts-de-France and INSA Hauts-de-France. My research focuses on ultrasonic wave propagation, passive imaging, and wave correlation in complex and reverberant media, with applications to the characterization of thin films, multilayer systems, and advanced materials. Since 2022, I have been serving as Chair of the Ultrasounds Technical Committee of the European Acoustics Association (EAA).

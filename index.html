Welcome to my website to learn more about my research and teaching activities!

Lynda Chehami is an Associate Professor at Université Polytechnique Hauts-de-France and INSA Hauts-de-France. She works on ultrasonic wave propagation, passive imaging, and wave correlation in complex and reverberant media, for the characterization of thin films, multilayer systems, and advanced materials. Member of CNU 63, Chair of the ULTRASOUNDS technical committee (EEA), and responsible for the Physics-Chemistry curriculum for the licence degree.

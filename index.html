Welcome to my website to learn more about my research and teaching activities!

I work on ultrasonic wave propagation, passive imaging and wave correlation in complex and reverberant media for the characterization of thin films, multilayer systems and advanced materials. I am a member of CNU 63, President of the ULTRASOUNDS technical committee of the European Acoustics Association (EEA), responsible for L2/L3 Physics-Chemistry curriculum (SPA track since Sept 2025), and internship supervisor for L3 PC SPA track.

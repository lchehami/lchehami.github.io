abstract: >-
  This work investigates the transmission spectrum of sound through a phononic
  crystal subjected to liquid flow and highlights the influence of flow-induced
  effects on ultrasonic wave propagation in periodic structures. The study
  demonstrates the sensitivity of phononic crystal transmission properties
  to flow-induced perturbations.

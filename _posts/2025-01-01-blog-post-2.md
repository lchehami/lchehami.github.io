I am pleased to announce that the **EAA Technical Committee on Ultrasounds**, which I currently chair, will actively contribute to the scientific program of **Forum Acusticum 2026**, taking place in Graz, Austria, from 8–12 September 2026.

Forum Acusticum is the annual conference of the European Acoustics Association (EAA) and represents one of the major international events in acoustics, gathering researchers, engineers, and industry experts from across Europe and beyond. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

The Ultrasounds Technical Committee will organize and support several scientific sessions dedicated to topics including:

- Nondestructive Testing and Evaluation (NDT&E)  
- Structural Health Monitoring (SHM)  
- Passive imaging and ambient noise correlation  
- Guided waves and ultrasonic sensing  
- Ultrasound sensor networks  
- Wave propagation in complex and reverberant media  
- Innovative ultrasonic imaging approaches  

These sessions will promote interdisciplinary exchanges between academia and industry while highlighting recent advances in wave physics, imaging, and monitoring techniques for complex structures.

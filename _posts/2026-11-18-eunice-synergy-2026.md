I am pleased to announce the organization of the **EUNICE Synergy 2026 International Congress**, which will take place on **18–19 November 2026** at the **Université Polytechnique Hauts-de-France (UPHF)** in Valenciennes, France.

This international congress will gather researchers, institutional representatives, industrial partners, and students from across the **EUNICE European University alliance** around major societal and scientific challenges related to:

- Sustainable mobility  
- Energy transition  
- Climate and environmental resilience  
- Smart engineering and digital technologies  
- Interdisciplinary innovation and European collaboration  

The event will feature:

- International keynote lectures  
- Scientific workshops and thematic sessions  
- Poster sessions for PhD students  
- Matchmaking and B2B meetings  
- Round tables involving academic and industrial stakeholders  

A special **EUNICE High School Challenge 2026** dedicated to “Designing the Mobility of Tomorrow” will also be organized to promote innovation and sustainability among younger generations.

## Conference Venue

The congress will be hosted at the **BTMD / MOBILIUM building** on the Mont Houy campus in Valenciennes.

I am pleased to announce the organization of the **EUNICE Synergy 2026 International Congress**, which will take place on **18–19 November 2026** at the **Université Polytechnique Hauts-de-France (UPHF)** in Valenciennes, France.

The congress will bring together researchers, institutional partners, industry representatives, and students from the EUNICE European University alliance around major societal challenges related to:

- Sustainable mobility  
- Energy transition  
- Climate and environmental resilience  
- Innovative engineering and digital technologies.  

# Headers---

Hosted at the **BTMD / MOBILIUM building** on the Mont Houy campus, the event will include:

- International keynote lectures  
- Scientific workshops and thematic sessions  
- Poster sessions for PhD students  
- Matchmaking and B2B meetings  
- Round tables with academic and industrial stakeholders  

The congress also aims to strengthen collaborations across the ten EUNICE regions while promoting interdisciplinary and European research initiatives aligned with **Horizon Europe Cluster 5** priorities.

A special **EUNICE High School Challenge 2026** dedicated to “Designing the Mobility of Tomorrow” will also be organized to engage younger generations in sustainable innovation.

I am pleased to announce that the **EAA Technical Committee on Ultrasounds**, which I currently chair, will actively contribute to the scientific program of **Forum Acusticum 2026**, taking place in Graz, Austria.

The Ultrasounds Technical Committee will organize several scientific sessions dedicated to:

- Nondestructive Testing and Evaluation (NDT&E)  
- Structural Health Monitoring (SHM)  
- Passive imaging and ambient noise correlation  
- Guided waves and ultrasonic sensing  
- Ultrasound sensor networks  
- Wave propagation in complex and reverberant media  

These sessions will promote interdisciplinary exchanges between academia and industry while highlighting recent advances in ultrasonic imaging and wave-based monitoring techniques.

## Conference Venue

Forum Acusticum 2026 will be held at the Messecongress Graz and will gather researchers and engineers from the international acoustics community.

date: 2018-07-15

I was pleased to be invited for a two-day research visit at the I2M laboratory in Bordeaux by Prof. O. Poncelet and Dr. S. Rodriguez during the APY DAY event in July 2018.

During this visit, I gave an invited seminar on nondestructive defect inspection using distributed sensor networks and sources of opportunity, highlighting passive approaches for Structural Health Monitoring (SHM) under real operating conditions.

The seminar focused on innovative passive imaging and correlation-based methodologies as a step toward passive SHM in complex media.

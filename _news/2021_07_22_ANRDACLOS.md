I was honored to receive the ANR Young Researcher Grant (JCJC) for the DACLOS project.

This funding supports the development of innovative approaches in ultrasonic wave physics, passive imaging, and wave correlation in complex and reverberant media, with applications to advanced materials and structural characterization.

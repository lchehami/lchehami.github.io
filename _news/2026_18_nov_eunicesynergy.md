I was honored to co-organize the first edition of the EUNICE Synergy Congress, held in Valenciennes within the framework of the European University Alliance EUNICE.

The congress brought together researchers, students, institutional representatives, and socio-economic stakeholders around major challenges related to mobility, energy, climate, and sustainable innovation.

This event reflects the collective effort and enthusiasm of numerous colleagues, collaborators, and international partners involved in strengthening scientific and educational cooperation across Europe.
